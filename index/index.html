MISSION

The Saint Francis of Assisi College(SFAC) aims to develop and nurture its student abilities and skill so they may be able to unfold their hidden talents and make them well-rounded person, well-informed individuals who will become the future leaders, effective and productive members of their respective families, their community and country under the blessings and grace of the Almighty.

VISION

The Saint Francis of Assisi College(SFAC) Library is envisioned to provide the Franciscan Community with quality education through a "Learning Resource Center" equipped with complete relevant, up-to-date, and valuable reading materials/learning references and modern facilities.

PHILOSOPHY

It is in the spirit of a "True Franciscan" that our Library is committed to provide the Franciscan Community with quality education by providing a "Learning Resource Center" complete with reading materials and upgrade learning references.
